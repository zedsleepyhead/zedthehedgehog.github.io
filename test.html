Welcome to Scrapts!

  • Scrapts is a non-profit organization that allows you to upload resources for other Scratcher's to use, things like costumes, sprites, scripts, backdrops, sounds, and much more. Scrapts' purpose is to revive the now defunct Scratch Resources and OpenSprites.

    The Scratch Guidelines also apply here, so make sure that what you upload follows it, otherwise, you may get a ban.

    Please note that this is not affiliated with MIT or the Scratch Team in any way.
